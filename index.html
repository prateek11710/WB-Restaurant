The Art of Spices, Charcoal, and Smoke

At Spice & Charcoal, we believe that dining is not just about eating, but about experiencing a story. Our kitchen pays homage to the ancient recipes of the royal courts of India, blending them with modern culinary innovations.

Every dish is cooked in custom tandoors fueled by genuine coconut-husk charcoal, infusing a deep, unmatched smoky aroma that highlights the natural richness of fresh ingredients, rich cream, and handcrafted spices.
